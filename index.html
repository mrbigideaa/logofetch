Logo Fetch

Company logos for Google Slides™, in one click.

What Logo Fetch does

Logo Fetch is a Google Slides™ add-on that lets you instantly find and insert high-quality company logos into your presentations — no more Googling, screenshotting, or background-removing.

Type a company name or website domain in the sidebar. Logo Fetch searches a database of 10M+ brands and returns logo variants optimized for light backgrounds, dark backgrounds, and neutral layouts. Click any result to drop it onto the current slide, correctly sized and centered.

Features

  • Autocomplete search by company name or domain
  • Light-background and dark-background logo variants, plus symbols and wordmarks
  • One-click insert onto the active Google Slides™ slide
  • Automatic sizing (max 25% of slide width) and centering
  • PNG and SVG supported

Permissions & privacy

Logo Fetch requests only the permissions it needs to do its job:

  • View and manage your Google Slides™ presentations — used only to insert the logo you click onto the active slide.
  • Connect to an external service — used only to call the Brandfetch API for logo data.

Logo Fetch does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
