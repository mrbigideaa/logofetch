LogoFetch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What Logo Fetch Does

Logo Fetch is a Google Slides™ add-on that fetches company logos from the Brandfetch API and inserts them into your presentations.

Data We Collect

Logo Fetch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not store your search queries.
  • We do not access your Google account information beyond what is needed to insert images into your current presentation.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking.
  • We do not collect analytics.

Third-Party Services

Logo Fetch sends your search query (company name or domain) to the Brandfetch API to retrieve logo images. This is the only external service we communicate with. Please refer to Brandfetch's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Permissions

Logo Fetch requests the following Google permissions:

  • View and manage your Google Slides™ presentations — Required to insert logo images onto your slides.
  • Connect to an external service — Required to call the Brandfetch API to fetch logos.

Data Retention

No user data is retained. All logo fetching happens in real-time and nothing is cached or stored on our servers.
